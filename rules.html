Rules & Regulations

01. General Conduct

Mutual Respect: Toxic behavior, hate speech, racism, or harassment targeted at any individual or community group will result in an immediate and permanent blacklist.

Impersonation: Impersonating official staff, founders, or ranked players is strictly prohibited across all linked community networks and platforms.

02. Gameplay Integrity

Unfair Advantage: The use of unapproved clients, hardware macros, auto-clickers, cheat modifications, or double clicking methods designed to artificially manipulate or boost CPS thresholds is forbidden.

Exploitation: Intentional abuse of map glitches, game mechanic oversights, or network optimization exploits to gain parity advantages inside matches will void results.

03. Tier Testing Etiquette

Official Verifications: Tier evaluation requests must exclusively navigate through verified Official Testers. Unofficial testing sessions will not affect leaderboards.

Match Manipulation: Offering bribes, throw-trading matches, or staging outcomes with testing staff will result in comprehensive demotion and ban frameworks for all parties involved.
